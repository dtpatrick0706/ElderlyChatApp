***Look at the 'Chat' picture in the outline pic file***

Consists of
-Virtual Face
-Chatting Box

----Virtual Face----
covers a bit more than half of the screen of the phone

*For now, keep this area empty, just leave space for it

**the face will move according to the response the AI gives (to be implemented in the future)


----Chatting Box----
the leftover parts of the screen

-----------------------------------------Schedule Page------------------------------------------
***look at the 'schedule' picture in the outline pic file***

The background color is a very light yellow

Consists of
-Calender
-the day's agenda

----Calender----
The Calender has the month, date, and the day written on the top

it has the basic dates and each day's basic agenda written in the Calender


----the day's agenda----
It is like a small box on the bottom of the screen
it has the agenda for the day written by the chronological order

*the app rings the alarm whenever its time for a thing we set on the schedule


**the schedule for its elderly user will be able to be accessed by the caretaker and the user
